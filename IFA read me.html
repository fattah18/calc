how to use our calculator:

The list of the functions:

  • sin: the sinus function; calculate with radian.

  • cos: the cosinus function; calculate with radian.

  • tan: the tangeante function; calculate with radian.

  • xpo: the exponential function.

  • ln: the niperian logarithm function.

  • log: the decimal logarithm function.

  • acos: the arccosinus function; give the result in radian.

  • asins: the arcsinus function; give the result in radian.

  • atan: the arctanteante function; give the result in radian.

  • fact: the factorial function.

  • deco: to convert from the octal to decimal.

  • decb: to convert from the binary to decimal.

  • oct: to convert from the decimal to oct.

  • bin: to convert from the decimal to binary.

  • sqrt: the square root function.

  • cbrt: the cube root function.

  • cosh: the hyperbolique cosinus function.

  • sinh: the hyperbolique sinus function.

  • tanh: the hyperbolique tangeante function.

  • acosh: the hyperbolique arccosinus function.

  • asinh: the hyperbolique arcsinus function.

  • atanh: the hyperbolique arctangeante function.

  • vabs: the absolute value function.

  • pi: the number pi=3.14...

  • e: the euler number.

  • C: to delete everything.

note:

when you use the functions you should open and close parenthesis; you should put parameters between parenthesis!!
